Hailee Mae Sharkey-Traynor
artist
Hailee has been a tattoo artist for 4 years and resided in Philly for 12. Specializing in bold and balanced illustrative tattoos with influence in american traditional and vintage artworks, her art career started with attending the University of Arts majoring in illustration. 

While she enjoys tattooing a wide range of subjects, she often finds herself tattooing florals, butterflies, ladies, traditional designs, script, and ornamental pieces.  Her work is whimsical, including lots of bright and moody colors, but she also always finds enjoyment in black and grey designs.

Hailee prides herself in being an empathetic artist who always wants to create a safe atmosphere for her clients. You can find her at the shop Tuesdays-Saturdays.

To book an appointment with Hailee, visit https://venue.ink/haileemaeta
