Makayla
Lulevich
owner, artist
Makayla started tattooing in 2019 after a departure from art school. She specializes in american traditional, with her own fun illustrative twist on it.

She enjoys using bold colors, illustrative designs, and loves to take on custom projects. She also enjoys doing black and grey traditional just as much as color if that's more your interest.

Makayla is committed to bring you a personable, warm, and positive tattoo experience and is excited to tattoo you in her cozy shop that feels like home.

To book an appointment with Makayla, please send her an email at: makayla.tattoo@gmail.com
