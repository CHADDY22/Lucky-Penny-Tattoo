Lucky Penny Tattoo, located in the South Street Headhouse District, was established in 2026 by artist Makayla Lulevitch. We strive to bring you a professional, cozy, welcoming environment to settle down and get tattooed in. We specialize in bright, bold, clean tattoos.

We book by appointment, or feel free to walk in with your idea.

We have frequent guest artists, which you could find updated on our instagram.

Everybody is welcome here. Whether it's to walk in to say hi, browse flash, or get tattooed, we can't wait to meet you!
