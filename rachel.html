Rachel
Knabe
artist
Rachel has been tattooing full-time since 2020, specializing in bold, bright work rooted in American traditional tattooing.

Her style is heavily influenced by pioneers of modern tattooing such as Ed Hardy, Bert Grimm, and Sailor Jerry, while still leaving room for versatility and creative collaboration.

Rachel enjoys creating tattoos that balance softness and toughness, and she welcomes a wide range of styles and ideas from her clients.

Outside of tattooing, Rachel spends her time painting flash, creating stained glass, and relaxing with her cat, Yams.To book an appointment with Rachel, please complete the booking form at https://www.rachelktattoo.com/booking
