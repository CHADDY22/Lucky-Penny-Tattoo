Julia
Laufer
artist
Born in Pennsylvania, Julia’s interest in tattoos started after seeing her first DIY stick and poke at the county fair.

Fast forward, Julia has been handpoking and machine tattooing in Philadelphia since 2022 and completed her apprenticeship in 2025. She tattoos in a bold, American traditional style with influences from rubber hose animation and Pennsylvania folk art.

You can find her at the shop Wednesday-Saturday!

To book, email is preferred but DMs are welcome. Email at: knowdiceink@gmail.com
