How can I book an appointment?
You can browse our artists on our artist page to find the one that best suits you, and can then find preferred booking and contact info. If you're not sure which artist would best suit you, you can send an inquiry to luckypennyphilly@gmail.com and we will pair you with the right artist.

Where are you located & where can I park?
We are located in South Street headhouse! You can find us at the very end of South Street, pretty much South and Front St. There is plenty of metered street parking outfront, or you could park in the closest lot located at 511 S. Front St.

Can I get tattooed if I'm under 18?
No, you cannot. Please bring a valid ID to your appointment.

How much will my tattoo cost?
Our shop minimum is $100, and our artists handle their own pricing. Please reach out to your artist ahead of time to get an estimate as it varies based on size, detail, placement, and design.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Cash is preferred. If you need to use another form of payment, please check with your artist beforehand. Many accept venmo as another option.

How should I prepare for my appointment?
‍Please come well rested, hydrated, and well fed ahead of time. You are welcome to bring any drinks or snacks to your appointment, and we will have some provided if you've forgotten. Be sure to wear loose fitting, comfortable clothing that gives easy access to the area that is getting tattooed. You are also welcome to bring a change of clothes to change into before your appointment.

How should I care for my new tattoo?
